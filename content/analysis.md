# Content Analysis - FIFA World Cup 2026 Website

## Original Site Sections

### 1. Hero / Landing
- Animated banner with tournament video (#WEARE26)
- Real-time countdown (days, hours, min, sec until June 11)
- CTA: "View Matches"
- Promotional banner for FIFA Fan Festival

### 2. Featured News Carousel
- 4-6 articles in large image card format
- Categories: "Key Information", "FIFA World Cup", country articles
- Each card: hero image, category tag, title, "More info" link

### 3. Hospitality Section
- Banner with logo, title, description and CTA "Choose your package"
- Premium luxury content

### 4. Featured Stories
- Horizontal scrollable grid with articles
- First card large, rest smaller (horizontal layout)
- "View all" link to full category

### 5. Explore More
- 5-item carousel with image + title:
  - Team Profiles
  - 26 Superstars
  - Follow the Countdown
  - Wonderful Goals
  - Test Your Knowledge

### 6. Teams Carousel (48 nations)
- Cards with: Flag, Name, Group, World Ranking, Appearances
- First 3 highlighted as "Host Country" (Canada, USA, Mexico)
- Rest in alphabetical order

### 7. Video Section (#WEARE26)
- Embedded video with Mute/Play-Pause controls
- Full-width section

### 8. Footer
- Host Countries links
- Official Documents
- Upcoming Tournaments & Events
- Social: Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok
- Apps: Google Play, Apple App Store
- Partners in 3 tiers (FIFA Partners, Sponsors, Supporters)

## Host Cities Page Structure

- Official Match Ball banner (adidas TRIONDA)
- Interactive map by country (separate iframes for Canada, Mexico, USA)
- Each country shows cities as map points
- CTA for parking reservation

### Cities (16 total):
- Canada (2): Toronto, Vancouver
- Mexico (3): Guadalajara, Mexico City, Monterrey
- United States (11): Atlanta, Boston, Dallas, Houston, Kansas City, Los Angeles, Miami, New York/NJ, Philadelphia, San Francisco Bay Area, Seattle

---

## Individual City Page Structure

- Hero video of the city with controls
- Tagline title (e.g. "Mexico City: The Stadium of Legends")
- Long descriptive paragraph about city, culture, fun facts
- City host logo/badge
- Matches in [City]: Match card carousel with tournament, stage, group, stadium, date, teams (with flags), time, broadcaster info
- Teams in [City]: Article carousel of teams playing there
- CTAs: Tickets, Hospitality
